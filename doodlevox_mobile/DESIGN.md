# Design System Document

## Theme: Midnight Sun

This document outlines the core visual style of our Midnight Sun theme, designed for a high-contrast, bold digital experience.

### Color Palette

The color palette is built around a striking gold, complemented by stark white and deep black, with a subtle grey accent. This monochrome variant ensures consistency and focus.

*   **Primary Color:** #FFB800 (A vibrant gold, used for key interactive elements and branding.)
*   **Secondary Color:** #FFFFFF (Pure white, for contrast and highlighting important content.)
*   **Tertiary Color:** #888888 (A neutral grey for subtle accents or disabled states.)
*   **Neutral Color:** #000000 (Absolute black, forming the dominant background for a dark mode experience.)

### Typography

The Inter font family is used consistently across all textual elements to maintain a modern and cohesive look.

*   **Headline Font:** Inter (Clean and highly legible for titles and headers.)
*   **Body Font:** Inter (Optimal for reading large blocks of text.)
*   **Label Font:** Inter (Clear and concise for UI labels and interactive elements.)

### Shape and Form

Our UI elements feature sharp, angular corners, reflecting a precise and modern aesthetic.

*   **Roundedness:** 0 (Sharp, angular corners for all UI components.)

### Spacing

The design incorporates a normal level of spacing between and within UI elements, balancing density with readability.

*   **Spacing:** 2 (Normal spacing, providing a comfortable visual flow without feeling too cramped or overly spacious.)

### Dark Mode

The theme is primarily designed for a dark mode experience, leveraging deep blacks and high-contrast gold and white for optimal visibility and reduced eye strain in low-light environments.
